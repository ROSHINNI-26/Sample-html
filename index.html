What is Black Goose Bistro?

The Black Goose Bistro is a restaurant in Seekonk, Massachusetts that serves casual lunch and dinner in a relaxed setting.


A casual restaurant that serves lunch and dinner in a hip atmosphere. The menu changes regularly to feature fresh local ingredients. Black Goose Bistro also offers catering for events like corporate fundraisers and bridge club meetings. It's open Monday–Thursday 11 AM–9 PM and Friday–Saturday 11 AM–midnight.
